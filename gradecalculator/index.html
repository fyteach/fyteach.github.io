Overall Grade Calculator

How to use the grade calculator

  • Enter the weights for each assessment (use the same scale).
  • Enter your scores.
  • Delete a row or leave the weight and score boxes empty to exclude an assessment.
  • Click "Add Row" to include more assessments.
  • Click "Calculate Grade" to see your overall grade.
  • To estimate the minimum average needed for the remaining assessments, leave the score boxes blank, enter your expected overall score (e.g., 74 for a C), and click "Estimate Missing Scores".
